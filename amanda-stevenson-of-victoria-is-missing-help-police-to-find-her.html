Amanda Stevenson of Victoria is missing, help Police to find her

Posted by Martina Birk on Monday, July 22, 2024

The disappearance of Amanda Stevenson, a well-known 19-year-old resident of Victoria, Texas, has sparked a wave of concern and urgency within the close-knit community. Last seen on November 19, Amanda’s sudden vanishing act has left the town on edge, prompting the Victoria Police Department to issue a missing person alert and seek public assistance in locating her.

Described as a young woman with black hair and brown eyes, Amanda stands at an average height and weighs approximately 125 pounds. The small community has rallied together in a citywide search, driven by a collective hope to bring the vibrant and kind-hearted Amanda back home to her anxious family.

The Victoria Police Department, recognizing the gravity of the situation, has called upon locals to actively participate in the search efforts for the missing teenager. Circulating a flier with Amanda’s physical details, they encourage anyone with information to contact Victoria Crime Stoppers.

Amy G. Salinas shared a post on fb for Amanda:

My heart and prayers go out to Amanda Stevenson’s mother, brother and entire family. She was found deceased . I was in their group Find Amanda Stevenson and while some of us prayed , an army was out searching for her. Thankful she was found and the family will have closure but the outcome is so very sad. Lord Jesus wrap your arms around her family during this difficult time. 😭🙏🏼🙏🏼🙏🏼🙏🏼🙏🏼

In a bid to expedite Amanda’s safe return, the police assure the confidentiality of any provided information and highlight the potential eligibility for a monetary reward. This emphasizes both the seriousness of the situation and the community’s commitment to ensuring Amanda is reunited with her loved ones.

As the search for Amanda Stevenson continues, the Victoria community remains resolute in their determination to find her, shedding light on the unwavering support and dedication present in times of uncertainty. The mysterious circumstances surrounding Amanda’s disappearance have intensified the collective efforts to bring her home and restore a sense of security to the tight-knit town.
