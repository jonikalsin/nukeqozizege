How many siblings does Dolly Parton have left alive?

Posted by Aldo Pusey on Tuesday, August 27, 2024

Dolly Rebecca Parton, 76, is on the news after being voted into the Rock & Roll Hall of Fame, despite previously asking to “respectfully bow out” of contention for the honor. She is believed to be one of the American songwriters with success as a songwriter for others.

She is said to be of only seven female artists to win the Country Music Association’s Entertainer of the Year Award.

In this article, we will be diving into her personal life, especially her siblings.

How many siblings does Dolly Parton have left alive?

Dolly, born on January 19, 1946, in Sevier County, Tennessee, United States is from a family of 12 siblings who were all born to Robert Lee Parton and Avie Lee Owens.

All 12 siblings of the Parton family have been identified as Willadeene, David Wilburn, Coy Denver, Dolly Rebecca Parton, Robert Lee, Stella Mae, Cassie Nan, Randle Huston, Larry Gerald, twins Floyd Estel, and Frieda Estelle, and Rachel Ann.

Unfortunately for the family, not all the siblings are alive as of May 5, 2022. It is on record that, three of the 12 siblings are deceased. Randle Huston, Larry Gerald, and one of the twins Floyd Estel are the three deceased.

Willadsen, David Wilburn, Coy Denver, Dolly Rebecca Parton, Robert Lee, Stella Mae, Cassie Nan, Frieda Estelle, and Rachel Ann are the nine of 12 siblings who are alive.

Dolly is a recipient of several awards including 11 Grammy Awards, 10 Country Music Association Awards, five Academy of Country Music Awards, four People’s Choice Awards, and three American Music Awards among others.
