How much does Chez Reavie  make a year?

Chez Reavie won the Barracuda Championship on Sunday (July 17), holding on in the windy final round of the PGA Tour’s lone modified Stableford scoring event for his third Tour victory.

Reavie, who was six points ahead entering the day, had a six-point round for a one-point victory over Alex Noren on Tahoe Mountain Club’s Old Greenwood layout.

Reavie became the first PGA TOUR winner aged 40 or over since Lucas Glover in the 2021 John Deere Classic a year ago. The Arizona player finished with 43 points. He also won the 2008 RBC Canadian Open and the 2019 Travelers Championship.
