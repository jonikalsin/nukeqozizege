Table of Biography

Isaiah Fredericks is a famous American YouTube star. Isaiah Fredericks is known for hilarious videos on his YouTube channel. He is a young star of the popular show Awesomeness TV.

Early Life and Education

Isaiah Fredericks was born on August 8, 2006, in Tacoma, Washington, United States. As of the year 2022, he is currently 16 years old. Currently, he lives in Los Angeles, California. He holds an American nationality and his birth sign is Leo. His father is Kevin Fredericks, a comedian known nest by his KevOnStage alias on his social media and his mother’s name is Melissa Fredericks. He has a younger brother named Josiah Fredericks AKA Jo-Jo, who is also well-known on social media. Moreover, he is still a school student as of 2020. However, he has not shared details about his school background and in which grade he is studying.

Isaiah Fredericks – Career

Isaiah has appeared on one of the popular shows Awesomeness TV and the Nickelodeon sketch comedy show Crazy I Say. He landed the role of Buckwheat in a new adaptation of The Little Rascals titled The Little Rascals Save the Day. It is one of the best movies so far. He first started his career on YouTube, when he begged his father, Kevin, to make him a video.
