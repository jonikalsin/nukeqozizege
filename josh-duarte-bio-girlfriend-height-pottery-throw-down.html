Meet Pottery Throw Down's Josh Duarte! His Bio, Girlfriend, Height, IG

Posted by Martina Birk on Friday, August 23, 2024

Channel 4’s “The Great Pottery Throw Down” has turned the heads of viewers by introducing skilled potters to the show. In 2022 also we have 12 talented casts who will be competing against each other to win the title. One of the contestants is Josh Duarte.

This Josh Duarte Bio covers information on his girlfriend, height, and his appearance on the show, and his castmate.

Josh Duarte On The Great Pottery Throw Down

Josh Duarte is one of the 12 participants who will compete in Channel 4’s The Great Pottery Throw Down in 2022.

The show first aired in 2015 and received a lot of positive feedback from viewers. It’s now in its sixth season, and you’ll meet judges Keith Brymer Jones and Rich Miller, as well as hosts Siobhán McSweeney and Ellie Taylor. Besides the returning hosts and judges you’ll also meet 12 full-time/part-time pottery enthusiasts who have something to show to the world.

AJ Simpson, Anna McGurn, Cellan, Bill Thomas, Jenny Cobb, Christine Cherry, Lucinda Lovesey, Miles Johnson, Nick Robatto, Tom Demeranville, and Zahra Jabir will all appear alongside Josh.

The show’s first episode was released on 2 January 2022 on Channel 4. Rose Schmits is the pottery technician for The Great Pottery Throw Down 2022, after taking over from Rich Miller last year. She came from the Netherlands and says the practice of pottery is “a metaphor for the trans experience”. Her pottery takes all different forms and often features abstract shapes with trans activism slogans.

The show was filmed in Stoke-on-Trent which is best known for its pottery industry.

Josh Duarte Girlfriend

Josh Duarte is an extremely private individual. Unlike most of his fellow contestants, he has kept his social media private so an attempt to learn about his personal life has proven futile because of that. Even on his “The Great Pottery Throw Down” bio for 2022, he talked about his job but didn’t share anything about being in a relationship.

So, we have to conclude that Josh is single as of this writing based on what we know. But, chances remain that he might be maintaining a dating life away from the life in social media.

How Tall Is Josh Duarte?

Speaking of his height, Josh stands tall above the height of 5 feet 10 inches.

Is Josh Duarte On IG, Facebook?

Yes, Josh is on Instagram as well as Facebook. As already shared, his socials are private at this moment. Moreover, his Instagram account (@joshduarte94) gathered 268 followers. His Facebook account is (@joshua.duarte.16) that doesn’t hold much information.

Josh Duarte Age

When the show “The Great Pottery Throw Down” was filmed, Josh Duarte was 27 years old.

Josh Duarte Job

Josh Duarte has built his professional career as a design and technology teacher. As a qualified teacher, he is currently working at Elstree Prep School in West Berkshire. (patchhawaii.org) According to the UK. Talent, Design, and Technology teacher makes an average of £34,455 per year.

He has also been working as an assistant housemaster for more than five years. Moreover, Josh has also been teaching at summer school at Marlborough College Summer School for teenagers 13 to 17 years. Besides pottery, he also teaches sports and loves playing them. Moreover, he also enjoys good food. When he taught in summer school, Josh looked forward to bringing all of the experiences into creating a fantastic environment for the Youth Associations and Teens group.

Outside of teaching design and technology, he is interested in pottery. Josh has been working with woods, metals, and ceramics since he was 11 years old. Most of the designs that he makes come from his Portuguese heritage.

Talking about education, Josh holds B.A. (Hons).
