Lin-Manuel Miranda Wife Vanessa Nadal Has Tested Positive For Covid-19

Posted by Larita Shotwell on Friday, April 26, 2024

 Lin-Manuel Miranda recently announced that his wife Vanessa Nadal has tested positive for Covid-19, while his children have tested negative.

Vanessa Nadal- All About Lin-Manuel Miranda’s wife

Vanessa Nadal is a well-known scientist and attorney. She graduated from the Massachusetts Institute of Technology with a bachelor’s degree in engineering and the Fordham University School of Law with a law degree.

Moreover, she interned at Johnson & Johnson, Jones Day, and King Ramsey Perry & Howell, LLP, among other businesses. She went on to work as an associate at Jones Day and pursued dual careers in medical research and law.

Nadal has experience in a variety of legal fields, including civil RICO lawsuits and the Fair Credit Reporting Act. She is known for her work on federal, state, and international matters.

She is 39 years old and was born on August 18, 1982. For the time being, her parents’ names are unknown.

Lin-Manuel Miranda And Vanessa Nadal’s Relationship

Vanessa Nadal and Lin-Manuel Miranda were both students at Hunter College High School and never dated as adolescents. During their wedding in 2010, the New York Times claimed that the former was unable to communicate with Nadal throughout his final year.

Vanessa’s Facebook profile was discovered by the actor in 2005. He invited her to a Freestyle Love Supreme Show, but she didn’t think Miranda was interested in her despite her attendance. He didn’t talk to her the entire night; she told The New York Times because it was a large gathering.

The rapper was able to obtain Vanessa’s phone number from one of his pals, and she soon recognized she was falling for him during a Miranda performance on another program.

After the concert, the two stood outside, and Nadal compared traffic to her favorite video game, Grand Theft Auto. In an interview with The New York Times, Lin-Manuel Miranda said:

“‘All right, you’re coming over to my place tonight, and we’re going to play Grand Theft Auto, watch the Jay-Z movie, and listen to Marc Anthony,’ I stated calmly… After that, we swiftly exchanged keys to our respective residences.”
