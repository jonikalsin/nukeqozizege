Madelyn Cline- Biography

Madelyn Cline is a South Carolina native who was born in Charleston. She is a professional actor best known for her role as Sarah Cameron in the Netflix series Outer Banks, which premiered in 2020. She can also be seen in Stranger Things, The Originals, and Vice Principals.

What is the Net Worth of Madelyn Cline? Salary, Earnings

Madelyn has an estimated net worth of $800,000 US dollars. She is a professional actor, thus this is her principal source of money. Cline has been in the film and television industries for many years. She has done some modeling and has been seen endorsing many products. Madelyn is poised to soar to new heights because of her fantastic acting abilities and gorgeous form.

Madelyn Cline- Birth, Age, Ethnicity, Siblings, Education

Cline was born on December 21, 1997, in Charleston, South Carolina, the United States, to engineers Mark Cline and Pam Cline. She was raised in Charleston and attended Pinewood Preparatory School.

She went to Coastal Carolina University but dropped out halfway through because she wanted to pursue acting professionally and moved to Los Angeles. Her parents did not initially embrace her decision, but they have now accompanied her to success.

Madelyn Cline- Relationship, Boyfriend

Madelyn has confirmed her on-screen affair with Outer Banks co-star Chase Stokes. They’ve been dating since June 2020 and have been posting images of themselves together on social media.

Madelyn Cline- Professional Career

When Madelyn went to casting calls in 2008, she was 11 years old and was chosen as an extra in Nailed, which was renamed Accidental Love in 2015. She spent her summers in New York, modeling and working for agencies and companies such as T-Mobile and Sunny D. Madelyn also appeared in commercials on television. She first appeared as Matilda in Milites Christi in 2009, and she has since appeared in 23rd Psalm: Redemption in 2011, Children of Wax in 2012, and Bridge of Gap in 2014.

Madelyn had to struggle at first when she moved to Los Angeles to pursue acting because she didn’t have a job or money with her. She dropped out of university to pursue a career as an actor. Cline was also training to be a professional dancer; she eventually received dance lessons and even completed an advanced ballet course. Cline made her television film debut in 2016 with her performance in The Jury. She was later seen in Savannah Sunrise and the Netflix smash series Stranger Things. The same year and the following year, she appeared in The Originals as Jessica and Vice Principals as Taylor Watts.

Widespread Recognition

She was cast as a lead in Netflix’s adolescent drama Outer Banks in 2020, with her on-screen and off-screen boyfriend Chase Stokes, where she played Sarah Cameron. This role was a significant accomplishment in Madelyn’s acting career, and she garnered a lot of attention after the series launched on April 15, 2020. She is also preparing to feature in What Breaks the Ice and Once Upon a Time in Staten Island, both of which will be published later this year, opposite Frank Grillo.

She is also expected to appear in a Hot Stuff music video by Kygo and Donna Summers with her boyfriend, Chase Stokes. Madelyn returned to Netflix for the second season of Outer Banks and captured the hearts of all her fans all over again. In an interview, she also hinted that Outer Banks might return for a third season, though she didn’t mention anything about the production timeline or release date.
