Has Sahara Ray Undergone Plastic Surgery? Her Dating History

Posted by Larita Shotwell on Sunday, May 5, 2024

Sahara Ray is an IG sensation who has always been in the limelight for dating different A-list celebrities. Even now, she’s making headlines for kissing Mod Sun!

So, is she all-natural? Who has she dated in the past? And what’s her net worth? Find answers as you scroll down.

Sahara Ray Dating History

Sahara Ray might have shared a kiss with Mod Sun, but they were not dating. “Mod is enjoying being single for the first time in years and having fun. He’s not in a relationship with anyone,” Mod Sun’s rep said.

The duo was seen locking lips in Sahara’s now-deleted Instagram Story.

But that wasn’t the only time, Sahara and Mod Sun spent some quality time together. Before this, Sahara also posted a video over the weekend of herself sunbathing on Mod Sun’s back patio while he painted. Later, they enjoyed a group dinner with friends where the Saharal captured a video of smiling Mod Sun, sitting next to her at the table.

Prior to Mod Sun, the OnlyFans model was linked with Luke Hemmings, back when the latter had just split from YouTube star Arzaylea Rodriguez in mid-2017. At the time, Luke made an IG post from the top of Griffith Observatory in Los Angeles tagging Sahara in the caption, writing,  “Adventure time @sahara_ray.”

Sahara responded by commenting on the post: “Stop being so perfect.’”

Luke is now engaged to Sierra Deaton, after four years of dating.

Just a few months before Sahara and Luke made the news, she was romantically linked with Jordan Barrett. At the time, Sahara even shared their steamy snaps on IG where she had positioned herself on top of the model. The pair were long longtime friends, having appeared together in other photos shared on social media. 

Two years after this, Sahara and Jordan again made headlines as they hit Miami Beach in May 2019. This time, Sahara raised eyebrows sporting a prominent love bite on her neck.

Surprisingly, Jordan came out of the closet in July 2021 as he announced his engagement with a fellow male model Fernando Casablancas. The next month, the duo got married in an intimate ceremony in Ibiza, Spain.

Now, coming back to Sahara’s dating history, she was spotted skinny dipping in Hawaii with Justin Bieber, a year before she was linked with Jordan. Later, Sahara also went topless for a photoshoot.

Finally, before Justin, she was rumored to have dated Cody Simpson. They were believed to have coupled up in 2015 after Cody split from Gigi Hadid. However, Cody shot down the rumors when he insisted on the Kylie and Jackie O Show that the two were just friends — though he admitted she was a “babe.”

Cody is now dating the Olympian Emma McKeon. Together, they also shared a $ 1,826 million home on the Gold Coast.

Who Are Sahara Ray Parents?

As for her mother, Gretchen @gretchen_gia_bach is a realtor and the found of CoastWoods Real Estate. She is a  Los Angeles, California native who attended the University of Santa Cruz.

Talking about her siblings, Sahara has half-sisters named Violet Edwards @viol3t_edwards and Eden Edwards @edenedwards from her mother’s side, and half-siblings named Ruby Ray, Tasman, and Addis from her father’s side.
