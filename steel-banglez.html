Steel Banglez Age & Early Life

Steel Banglez was born on February 5, 1987, into a Sikh household in Forrest Gate, London, United Kingdom. He is 36 years old as of 2023. His mother was a music instructor, and his father was a poet, so he grew up surrounded by traditional Indian instruments like harmoniums and tablas. Steel Banglez’s actual name is Pahuldip Singh Sandhu, and his nickname was given to him by a Jamaican friend in honor of the karas (steel bracelets) he wears as a Sikh.
