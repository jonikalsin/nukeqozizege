Who is T. J. Lavin?

T. J. Lavin is an American BMX rider, Musician, Host, and TV Personality. He is Better known as the host of MTV’s The Challenge since its eleventh season.

T. J. Lavin: Age, Parents, Siblings, Education

T. J. Lavin was born on December 7, 1976, in Las Vegas, Nevada, United States.

As of 2023, he is 47 years old. He holds an American nationality and his ethnicity is unknown. His full name is Thomas Joseph Lavin and his Zodiac sign is Sagittarius.

The host mother’s name is Barbara Lavin. He is raised by a single mother, and Lavin never mentions his father. He has a sister named Brooke Lavin.

Lavin is so close to his mother and sister also, that he shares their photos on his social media.

Lavin first began ride bikes when he was two. He raced BMX until he was 15 then he gave up racing to try dirt jumping.

T. J. Lavin was also on the wrestling team while in high school. Details regarding his academic background are unavailable.

T. J. Lavin: Professional Life, Career

T. J. became a professional in dirt jumping at the age of nineteen. He has won almost every competition he has been entered including X Games and the Dew Action Sports Tour. In 1995, he has crowned the king of Dirt.

On October 14, 2010, he was competing in a Dew Tour event in Las Vegas when he crashed and was rushed to the hospital. Due to the critical injuries, he is in a coma for nine days. Besides rider, he has a recording studio in his home.

He released his album called LAVS the first SET. He appeared on the song “Never Gonna Hold Me Down” along with Johnny Richter on the album Sub Noize Soulijaz, and on the song “Watcha Want” on the album Random Stuff.

Similarly, he has hosted spin-offs, specials, and MTV’s reality-competition show The Challenge since its eleventh season.

T. J. Lavin is the subject of the film A Film about T. J. Lavin. In 2012, he appears in a starring role as Eli in the Independent film A Monster Among Men.
