What is Yeshua (Dance Cruise)?

Yeshua (dance cruise) is the remix of the famous song Yeshua by Jesus Image Choir. The song which was a solemn song with an excellent instrumental was remixed into a fast and danceable song with other additional words in it. The original song has a nice rhythm that makes you feel the warmth and embrace of the supernatural, connecting you to God in awe of His name.

Yeshua song is one of the most popular songs by the group as it draws you to dwell on the beauty of God Himself and it seeks to declare the majesty and beauty of the one who created the heavens and the earth. Meanwhile, the meaning of the word Hebrew word “Yeshua” means “Jesus” which further means to save or to deliver.
